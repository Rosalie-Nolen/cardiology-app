# Heads Up for Cardiology

Heads Up for Cardiology is an engaging group game designed 
to reinforce clinical knowledge in medical students and residents. 

## About

Heads Up for Cardiology brings the popular Heads Up game format to clinical 
education. One player holds the phone to their forehead with the clue facing 
the group, while other players give hints to help them guess the cardiology 
term or concept before the 30-second timer runs out.

## Features

- Timed 30-second flashcard rounds
- Curated cardiology flashcard deck with explanations and cited sources
- Swipe up to skip a card, swipe down to reveal the explanation
- Add and save your own custom cards to personalize your deck
- Fully offline — no account or internet connection required

## How to Play

1. Open the app and swipe up on the welcome screen to begin
2. The guesser holds the phone against their forehead facing the group
3. The group gives hints to help the guesser identify the cardiology term
4. Swipe up to skip to the next card or swipe down to reveal the answer
5. Cards advance automatically after 30 seconds

## Disclaimer

This app is designed for educational purposes only and is not intended to 
replace clinical judgment or professional medical advice.
