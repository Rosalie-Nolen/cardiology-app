# Privacy Policy

**Heads Up for Cardiology**
Last updated: March 2026

## Overview

Heads Up for Cardiology is an educational flashcard application designed for 
medical students and residents. This privacy policy explains how 
the app handles your information.

## Information We Collect

Heads Up for Cardiology does not collect, transmit, or share any personal 
information. The app operates entirely offline and does not require an account, 
login, or internet connection.

## Data Storage

The only data stored by this app is any custom flashcards you choose to create 
yourself. This data is stored locally on your device using Apple's standard 
UserDefaults storage system and never leaves your device. It is not transmitted 
to any server, third party, or external service.

## Third Party Services

Heads Up for Cardiology does not integrate any third party analytics, 
advertising, tracking, or data collection services.

## Children's Privacy

This app is intended for use by medical students and healthcare professionals 
and is not directed at children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy

Any future changes to this privacy policy will be reflected on this page with 
an updated date. Continued use of the app following any changes constitutes 
acceptance of the revised policy.
